How It Works

1

Extract Content

Click the extension icon on any webpage. WebPodify intelligently extracts the main content using Readability.js, filtering out ads and navigation.

2

AI Conversion

The T5-small AI model (running in your browser) converts the web content into a natural 2-person dialogue format with smooth transitions.

3

Listen & Enjoy

Web Speech API synthesizes the dialogue with two distinct voices. Control playback speed, pause anytime, and enjoy your personalized podcast.
